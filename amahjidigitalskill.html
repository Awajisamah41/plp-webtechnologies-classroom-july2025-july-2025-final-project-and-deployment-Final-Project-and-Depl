The Challenges We Address

Practical Skill Gap

Many graduates lack job-ready digital skills, creating a disconnect between education and employment opportunities.

Limited Career Opportunities

High unemployment rates, particularly in Northeast Nigeria, limit career paths for young adults.

Gender Digital Divide

Girls and young women often face limited access to crucial digital education, widening the gender gap in tech.

Lack of Opportunity

Less privileged individuals struggle to gain the essential digital skills needed for modern employment.

Slow Digital Transformation

Businesses in the region lag in adopting digital technologies and new learning methods, hindering growth.

Our Unique Value Proposition

Practical Skills & Empowerment

We bridge the gap between theory and practice, empowering underserved communities (girls & less privileged) in Northeast Nigeria with actionable digital skills.

Holistic Career Support

Beyond training, we offer comprehensive career development and job placement services to ensure our students succeed.

Local Impact, Global Vision

We drive digital transformation in the Northeast with a commitment to international standards, aiming to make a significant impact across Africa.

Our Comprehensive Solution:

Amahji Digital Skill Bridge Techy provides practical, hands-on digital skills training and robust career services. We dedicate resources to empower girls and the less privileged, while also driving digital transformation for businesses through specialized training and consulting.

About Amahji Digital Skill Bridge Techy

Founded by Amah A. Dandison, Amahji Digital Skill Bridge Techy holds a first-mover position in addressing the unique needs of underserved communities in Northeast Nigeria.
